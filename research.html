Research

Welcome to the research page of the department. Our department is committed to advancing the field of computer science through cutting-edge research in various areas. Our faculty members and researchers are actively engaged in pushing the boundaries of knowledge and innovation.



RESEARCH AREAS
1. Intelligence and Machine Learning.
Explore our contributions to the exciting fields of artificial intelligence and machine learning. Our researchers focus on developing intelligent systems, algorithms, and models that enhance human-computer interaction and decision-making processes.

2. Cybersecurity.
Discover the latest advancements in cybersecurity research. Our team is dedicated to addressing the evolving challenges of securing digital information and infrastructure. From cryptography to network security, our research aims to create robust solutions for a secure digital future.

3. Soft engineering
Delve into the world of software engineering research. Our faculty members work on improving software development processes, software quality, and reliability. Learn about our projects that contribute to the creation of efficient, scalable, and maintainable software.

4. Data science and Big data.
Explore the intersection of computer science and data science. Our researchers are actively involved in extracting meaningful insights from large datasets, developing data-driven models, and contributing to the field of big data analytics.

Publications.

Explore our latest publications, including conference papers, journal articles, and book chapters. Our researchers actively contribute to leading conferences and journals in the field, disseminating valuable knowledge and insights.
